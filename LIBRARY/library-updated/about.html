About Library

Our Dr. GRD Memorial Library has a rich collection of books, journals and e-resources catering to students, research scholars and faculty members. Presently, over 2.47 Lakhs of Print books, 12943 CDs/DVDs and 206 printed journals, 13 e-databases are possessed by the library access to more than 1075 online electronic journals and 2267 e-books. The library is automated and the resources can be easily accessed by the users through our online opac portal. The library implemented PSG-ILMS which is an integrated Library Management System that supports all housekeeping operations of the library. There is a separate Digital Library having 60 computers running on high speed internet. This library has provided a Remote Access facility to all our students and faculty members. We have a separate Mobile App facility named Exordium, Chat bot and a dynamic web page for library exclusively. The library system and staff is meticulously focused on providing a comfortable, user friendly environment to the students' knowledge creation.
